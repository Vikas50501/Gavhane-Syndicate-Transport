About Us

Maharashtra's Road Transport Leaders Since a Decade

Gavhane Syndicate Transport is a road transport organization with 28 fleets covering a wide network across Maharashtra. With a yearly turnover of ₹6 crore, we have gained expertise in handling transportation projects across Power & Oil, Steel & Cement, Textile & Engineering, Fertilizers & Chemicals, Petrochemicals, and Bulk Drugs industries. We offer trucks, tempos, close body trucks, side open trucks, 20ft & 40ft flat trailers for container handling, and ODC consignment services.
